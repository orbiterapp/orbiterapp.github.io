Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

Orbiter helps you capture and manage tasks. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data: name, email, and profile details from sign-in provider.
  • Task data: tasks, projects, reminders, and preferences you create in Orbiter.
  • Technical data: basic device/app diagnostics needed for reliability and security.

2. How we use information

  • Provide and sync Orbiter across your devices.
  • Authenticate your account and keep sessions secure.
  • Improve stability, performance, and product quality.

3. Storage and security

We use industry-standard protections for data in transit and at rest. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously work to protect your information.

4. Third-party services

Orbiter may rely on third-party infrastructure (for example, authentication and cloud data storage) to operate core features. Those providers process data according to their own policies and security controls.

5. Data retention

We retain data while your account is active and as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

6. Your choices

  • You can edit or delete your tasks in the app.
  • You can sign out at any time.
  • You can request account/data deletion by contacting support.

7. Children's privacy

Orbiter is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date on this page.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy: support@orbiterapp.com

If you do not agree with this policy, please stop using Orbiter.
